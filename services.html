Our Therapy Services

Acupressure Therapy

A gentle, hands-on technique that applies pressure to specific points on the body. It helps relieve pain, reduce stress, and restore energy flow.

Needling Therapy

Targets tight muscles and trigger points using fine needles. Provides quick relief and improves mobility.

Moxa Therapy

Uses heated herbal moxa near the skin to enhance circulation and support natural healing.

Cupping Therapy

Stimulates blood flow and eases muscle tension using suction cups.

Reflexology

Therapeutic foot massage focusing on pressure points connected to organs and systems.

Shiatsu Therapy

Japanese massage using finger pressure to promote deep relaxation and balance.
